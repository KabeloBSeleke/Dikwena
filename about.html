About Dikwena Pest Control

Going beyond expectations since 2024

Our Story

Founded in July 2024 by Leshabane Mabina and Leshabane Phumza, Dikwena Pest Control was born from a vision to provide safe, reliable, and professional pest control services to the people of Gauteng.

With over two years of hands-on experience as a Pest Control Officer at Hanani Pest Control, founder Leshabane Mabina brings certified expertise and a deep understanding of effective pest management.

Since our inception, we have received overwhelming support from businesses, homeowners, landlords, and tenants across Gauteng Province. Our commitment to excellence has earned us great reviews and countless referrals.
